Bio

A collaboration between producer and multi-instrumentalist
Adrian Quesada and singer/songwriter Eric Burton, Black
Pumas fuse cinematic neo-soul, light psychedelia and a touch
of urban grit. Rising from their studio origins to a hard-hitting
live act, the group released their debut album in 2019.
